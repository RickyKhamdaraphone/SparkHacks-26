Hou Minghao

Hou Minghao, also known as Neo Hou, is a Chinese actor and singer who has gained recognition for his diverse roles and captivating performances. He has starred in several successful dramas and films, including The Devotion of Suspect X, When We Were Young, The Lost Tomb 2, A Girl Like Me, and Our Times. His dedication to his craft is evident in his memorable performances, and he has collaborated with well-known directors and co-stars.
