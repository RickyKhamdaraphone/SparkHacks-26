Ben Wang

Ben Wang is the stage name of American actor Wang Pucun. He's known for his roles as Jin Wang in the Disney+ series American Born Chinese and Bo in Chang Can Dunk. In 2025, he starred as Li Fong in the film Karate Kid: Legends, where he performed combat choreography with Jackie Chan. Wang has said that he considers it an honor if young Asian American children see him as a role model.
